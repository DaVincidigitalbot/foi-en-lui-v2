## Printing Methods (ranked by quality)

### 1. Embroidery (BEST for Foi en Lui)
- **Actual thread stitching** — real texture you can feel
- Perfect for the shield logo and short text (1-3 words)
- Premium perceived value — customers pay $10-20 more
- Printful supports embroidery on select blanks
- **Use for:** Shield logo, "CHOSEN", "TRUST", single-word designs
- **Cost:** ~$5-8 per piece additional

### 2. DTG (Direct to Garment)
- Best for detailed designs, full verses, multi-color
- Print quality is excellent on cotton blanks
- Feels like part of the fabric after washing
- **Use for:** Full verse designs, detailed layouts
- **Cost:** Included in Printful base price

### 3. Puff Print / HD Vinyl (PREMIUM add-on)
- Raised 3D texture — looks and feels like embroidery
- Great for large text and bold designs
- **Not available on Printful** — would need local printer
- Future option when volume justifies it

## Quality Differentiators (what makes it NOT Walmart)

### Fabric
- ✅ Ring-spun cotton (softer than open-end cotton)
- ✅ Combed cotton (removes short fibers → smoother)
- ✅ Side-seamed construction (no twisting after wash)
- ✅ Tear-away labels (add your own woven Foi en Lui label later)
- ❌ No tubular construction (that's cheap Gildan territory)

### Finishing
- ✅ Garment-dyed options (Comfort Colors) — softer, vintage feel
- ✅ Double-needle stitched hems — durability signal
- ✅ Retail fit (not boxy) — Bella+Canvas nails this

### Branding & Packaging
- **Custom woven labels** — Printful offers these (Foi en Lui + shield)
- **Custom neck labels** — replace manufacturer tag with your brand
- **Tissue paper wrap** — $.50 per order, huge perceived value
- **Thank you card** — include a Bible verse card in every order
- **Sticker** — Foi en Lui shield sticker, costs $0.10 each

### Future Upgrades (when revenue supports it)

3. **Embroidered patches** sewn on select premium pieces
