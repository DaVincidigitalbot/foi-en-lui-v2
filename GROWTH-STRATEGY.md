# Foi en Lui — Growth Strategy & Business Plan

## The Vision
Foi en Lui isn't just a t-shirt shop. It's a faith-based lifestyle brand. The goal: become the Elevated Faith / Fear of God Sunday Service of embroidered scripture apparel.

## Phase 1: Foundation (NOW — Month 1-2)

### Product
- 24-piece launch collection (women, men, teens)
- Premium blanks only (Comfort Colors, Bella+Canvas)
- Real embroidery on select hero pieces
- Shield logo on everything

### Sales Channels
1. **Etsy** (DaVinciDigitalStore) — already set up
2. **Gumroad** — digital presence
3. **Instagram Shop** — direct checkout from posts
4. **TikTok Shop** — massive organic reach for faith content
5. **Foi en Lui website** — V2 is already live

### Marketing (FREE — Do These First)
1. **Instagram Reels/TikTok** — 3x/week
   - "Verse of the Day" wearing Foi en Lui
   - Behind-the-scenes of embroidery/printing
   - Unboxing videos
   - "What verse are you wearing today?"
   - Scripture + outfit of the day
2. **Pinterest** — Pin every product with verse + lifestyle image
   - Pinterest drives MORE Etsy traffic than any other platform
   - Create boards: "Faith Fashion", "Christian Hoodies", "Bible Verse Apparel"
3. **Facebook Groups** — join Christian women's groups, youth ministry groups
   - Don't spam — add value, mention products naturally
4. **Church connections** — Graydon's church, Emily's network, girls' teams
5. **Hashtag strategy:**
   - #FaithFashion #ChristianApparel #FoiEnLui #WearYourFaith
   - #BibleVerse #ScriptureTee #FaithBasedClothing #ChristianStreetWear

### Marketing (PAID — Start Small)
1. **Etsy Ads** — $3-5/day to start, test which designs get clicks
2. **Instagram/Facebook Ads** — $5-10/day targeting:
   - Christians 18-35
   - Interest: Bible, church, worship music, Christian influencers
   - Lookalike audience from website visitors
3. **Google Shopping Ads** — later, once you have reviews

## Phase 2: Build Community (Month 2-4)

### The "Faith Family" Community
- **Email list** — collect emails from website + Etsy
- **Weekly "Verse Drop"** email — featured verse + product + devotional
- **Ambassador Program:**
  - Give free products to 10-20 micro-influencers (1K-10K followers)
  - They post wearing it → you repost → social proof
  - They get 15% commission on sales through their code
  - Target: Christian college students, worship leaders, youth pastors

### Content Strategy
- **"Wear Your Verse" campaign** — customers share photos wearing their verse
- **User-generated content** is FREE marketing and builds trust
- **Collaborate with Christian podcasters** — send free merch, get mentioned
- **Spotify playlist** — "Foi en Lui Worship" playlist (drives brand awareness)

### Reviews & Social Proof
- First 50 orders: include handwritten note asking for Etsy review
- Offer 10% off next order for a photo review
- Screenshot every positive review for social media

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 4-8)

### Hiring (When Revenue Justifies It)

**1. Social Media Manager / Content Creator — $500-1000/month (part-time)**
- Find a Christian college student who's good at Reels/TikTok
- They create 3-5 posts per week
- They manage DMs and engagement
- Where to find: Christian universities, Fiverr, Instagram DMs to small creators
- **This is your #1 hire.** Content is everything.

**2. Influencer Manager — Commission only (0$/month)**
- Someone who manages your ambassador program
- They recruit influencers, send products, track codes
- Pay them 5% of ambassador-driven sales
- Could be a college intern for credit

**3. Graphic Designer — $200-500/month (contract)**
- Creates new designs monthly
- Produces social media graphics
- Designs seasonal collections
- Find on: Fiverr ($15-30/design), 99designs, or local art student

**4. NOT needed yet:**
- ❌ Warehouse/shipping (Printful handles this)
- ❌ Customer service person (you + DaVinci handle it)
- ❌ Accountant (use QuickBooks Self-Employed until $50K+ revenue)
- ❌ Lawyer (you have the LLC, legal page is done)

### Expand Product Line
- **Hats** — embroidered shield logo, huge margin
- **Crewneck sweatshirts** — between tee and hoodie price point
- **Tank tops** — summer collection
- **Joggers** — matching sets with hoodies
- **Beanies** — winter collection, embroidered "FEL" or shield
- **Tote bags** — "Carry your faith" with verse
- **Phone cases** — low cost, high margin on Printful
- **Stickers/Decals** — $1 to make, sell for $5-8, great add-on

### Seasonal Drops
- **Easter Collection** (April) — resurrection verses, spring colors
- **Back to School** (August) — teen-focused, campus wear
- **Fall/Thanksgiving** (October) — gratitude verses, earth tones
- **Christmas Collection** (November) — gift sets, holiday packaging
- **Valentine's** (February) — love verses, couples matching sets

## Phase 4: Brand Building (Month 8-12)

### Wholesale
- Approach Christian bookstores (LifeWay, Mardel, local shops)
- Youth ministry bulk orders (camps, retreats, mission trips)
- Church merchandise partnerships
- Wholesale pricing: 50% off retail for 24+ units

### Pop-Up Events
- Church events and conferences
- Christian music festivals (Creation Fest, Kingdom Bound)
- Local farmer's markets
- College campus events near Christian universities

### Website Upgrade
- Move from GitHub Pages to Shopify ($39/month)
- Full e-commerce with cart, checkout, inventory
- Blog with devotionals tied to each verse/product
- SEO for "Christian hoodies", "Bible verse shirts", etc.

### Collaborations
- **Worship artists** — design a collab collection with a Christian musician
- **Christian athletes** — send free products to college athletes with platforms
- **Church youth groups** — custom designs for their group + Foi en Lui branding
- **Mission organizations** — % of proceeds to missions = marketing + impact

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Authenticity** — built by a real family, not a faceless brand
2. **Embroidery** — most competitors do cheap DTG only
3. **Premium blanks** — you feel the difference immediately
4. **The shield** — distinctive brand mark (most faith brands have no logo identity)
5. **Daughters' involvement** — teen collection designed WITH teens, not FOR teens
6. **French name** — "Foi en Lui" is elegant and memorable, stands out from "Faith Over Fear" generic brands
7. **Surgeon credibility** — "Founded by a surgeon and his family" adds unexpected legitimacy
