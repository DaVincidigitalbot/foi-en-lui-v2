Our Story

A journey of faith, fashion, and purpose

The Vision

It started with a simple truth: faith should be worn boldly. Not hidden, but declared. Not whispered, but proclaimed through every thread.

The Name

Foi en Lui - "Faith in Him" in French. A reminder that our strength, our purpose, our very existence flows from one source: Christ.

The Mission

Every piece we create carries Scripture that has transformed lives. Your hoodie becomes a conversation starter. Your tee becomes a testimony.

The Future

This is just the beginning. Join us as we clothe the world in faith, one verse at a time.

Join the Family

Be the first to know about new designs, verse drops, and special releases. This isn't just a newsletter—it's a community of faith.
